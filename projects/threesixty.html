THE 360 PODCAST

Website | Express & EJS

The 360 is a podcast on Spotify that covers various subjects ranging from sports, general news, and life-stories. From The 360's website, users can link to all social media accounts associated with the podcast, as well as open up Spotify to go to the accounts Spotify profile. Not only does the website link users to these pages, but the website for the podcast hosts all the podcast episodes via a MongoDB database and Spotify Link embedding.
