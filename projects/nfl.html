NFL Fantasy Football Rank Predictor

Case Study | Pandas, Sci-Kit Learn, Keras

Using statisitics from 2011-2021 web-scrapped from Pro Football Reference, we break up that stats by offensive postion and use a neural network model to predict the top 10 ranked PPR (Points per reception) fantasy players at each postion (RB, QB, WR, TE). The stats used in the model were in-games stats such as reception yards, rushing years, touchdowns, fumbles, etc. Age, usage, and the previous PPR points were also used. To perform hyperparameter tuning, we used the keras package to build the models and cross-validate models with grid search to find the optimal parameters.
