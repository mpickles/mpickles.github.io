Single-Qubit Gates Visualizer

GUI Application | Python, Tkinter, & Qiskit

This Tkinter GUI application allows users to input a series of single-qubit gates and visualize those gates on a bloch sphere using Qiskit. Users choose from 11 different single-qubit gates and can input up to 10 different gates at a time. The visualizer goes through each inputted gate step-by-step and animates the associated bloch sphere rotation, allowing users to better understand the effects of the applying single-qubit gates on the state vector.
