My Journey To Where I Am Today

ABOUT ME

I graduated from the University of California San Diego in March of 2023 with a bachelor's degree in applied mathematics and a minor in Economics. I originally am from Washington State where I grew up on an alpaca ranch in the small town of Darrington, WA. I am a culinary enthusiast who enjoys cooking dishes from various cultures while watching Anthony Bourdain: Parts Unknown or listening to music. On Sundays, you can find me glued to the television watching NFL RedZone, keeping track of my fantasy team.

MY ACADEMIC JOURNEY

In the summer of 2015, I was a bored 15-year-old with nothing to do, so I thought it would be fun to learn how to program a 2D platformer game to sell on STEAM and get rich. I did not end up getting rich, but it sparked a strong interest in computer science. The following year I enrolled in my school's TEALS program with Microsoft and learned basic CS principles. I also discovered a strong interest in Math and Physics and was allowed to pursue independent engineering projects for credit. I experimented with building MAGLEV trains in a vacuum, optimizing a 12-foot piston-actuated vacuum cannon, and creating a plasma engine using radio waves to ionize argon gas in a vacuum to generate plasma.

Outside of classes, I spent time learning how to become a better coder, taking online courses, and watching YouTube videos whenever I could. Doing this allowed me to explore other topics I was interested in, such as UX design, blockchain applications outside of cryptocurrency, and quantum computing.

WHAT I'M DOING TODAY

In March 2023, I officially graduated from UCSD and moved to Berkeley, California where I currently reside. In this part of my life, I want to spend time learning practical skills and expanding my worldview. I continually practice my coding and data science skills while also working odd part-time jobs that allow me to meet and interact with a wide variety of people, as well as pursue more creative endeavors.

I do intend to work in the data science field and possibly pursue graduate studies in statistics or computer science, but right now my main goal is to become a well-rounded individual with a strong understanding of the world and plenty of stories to tell. I am constantly eager for what the future has in store, and excited for the new opportunities, adventures, and lessons that will come my way.
