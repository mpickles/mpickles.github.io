ABOUT THIS PROJECT

For this project, I was tasked with redesigning the website for North Counties' Family Services (NCFS), a non-profit organization based in Washington State. When looking at the old website, the old website was lacking an idenntiy. It contained basic information on how to contact them, flyers for events that they ran, and some photos of their community work. After several discussion identifying what the client wanted out of their website and the information they would be providing, I was given complete creative control.

The overall site theme used neutral colors with the main primary color being a dark green to represent the forests that surrounds their communities. I built a landing page that displayed their slogan and mission statment clearly with a background of a mother and child walking in a forest that represents the family services the client provides.

On the home page, their are several sections with links to pages with more information on the sections: Services and Programs, About Us, Upcoming Events, and a Contact section. I transfered all the relevent information from the old website to the new website while also adding new information that was provided to me from the client.
