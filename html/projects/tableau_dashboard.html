ABOUT THIS PROJECT

To prepare for the 2023 Fantasy Football season, I download multiple datasets from the 2022 NFL season from various sources such as FantasyPros and Pro-Football-Reference and combined them into one main dataset. The combined dataset has contained statistics from the top 300 relevant fantasy football players from the 2022 season, and contains information of each player, their team, average draft position, game stats, season stats, and other calculated metrics such as average points per game.

The dataset was then connected to Tableau to be used as the data source. In Tableau, I built 3 main visualizations: A table with the players' names, position, ranks, average points per games, and total points, a scatterplot comparing average points per game and a player's coefficient of variation, and another scatterplot comparing the average draft position and total points scored. These visualizations were combined into a single dashboard with multiple filters to allow users to explore the data.
